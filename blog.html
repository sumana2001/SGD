Speakers and Guests

Lisa Russells

Ms. Lisa Russell is an Emmy Award-winning filmmaker and Founder of Create2030 who has 15+ years experience producing films and creative events across the globe for leading UN/NGO agencies. An established global speaker and thought leader, Lisa is a two time TEDx speaker, SheDecides Global Champion, UN/NGO contracted storyteller and arts curator and Fulbright Specialist.

Wally Tham

Meet Mr. Wally Tham from Singapore, Director of “Sayang Kalimantan”. He is an Organisational Development practitioner as well as an award winning media producer. He also serves as the Director of Big Red Button Organization. How the filmmaker and his colleagues spent two years designing and building Indonesia’s first functioning haze shelter in the town of Kalimantan.

Diana Henriques

Ms. Diana Henriques is the Project Manager at Lisboa-E-Nova focusing on Education for Sustainable Development, Biodiversity, Climate Change, Circular Economy as well as citizen engagement and participatory activities. She has coordinated projects including Aquametragem / Aquastory (awarded within UN's SDGs in Action Film Festival in the Category "Protecting our planet).

Nicole Watson

Nicole shares her time between her non-profit program work and production of factual content in the film industry. Founder of ‘econic earth’ a 501(c)3 charitable organization based in California, she is dedicated to environmental and wildlife conservation initiatives while empowering students and communities with solar power.

Jack Sims

Globally known as Mr. Toilet, Mr. Sims is a Serial Entrepreneur with 16 businesses turned Serial Social Entrepreneur with 8 social businesses. He retired at the age of 40 to devote full time on changing the world. Mr. Sims had founded the Restroom Association of Singapore in 1998 and World Toilet Organisation in 2001. He is also accredited with Creating the UN World Toilet Day.


Inessa Kraft

Inessa Kraft is an actress, writer, and director. Sustainability, education, and connecting cultures are the main topics of her work and life. Inessa is focused on educational, travel, and inspirational films, videos, advertisements, and stories. She discovered a book of Konstantin Stanislavski "An Actor prepares" and was amazed that the art of acting is not about pretending or "acting mechanically," but actually about living.

Bethany Borg

Bethany Borg is the Director of the short film “The Conductor” and is from the United Kingdom. She has a degree in Animation and Media from the University of Wolverhampton and has been a part of several projects in several capacities and film making is something she looks up to as a way of creating change in the world.
