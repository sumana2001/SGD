FAQs

What is the SDG International Virtual Film Festival all about and who is organizing it?

The SDG International Film Festival aims to bridge the gap between policy makers, civil society, non-profits and the youth. Through the 2-day festival which would involve online screening of award-winning documentaries and short films based on the various SDG thematic areas as well as pre-conference talk series with various experts, we hope to make it an enriching experience for both the participants and other stakeholders.

Who can participate and what are the dates of the Film Festival?

Any School or College student is free to participate as a Delegate at the SDG International Film Festival. Working Professionals can also participate in the Festival by mentioning the name of their organization on the registration form

Is there a registration fee for attending the Film Festival? Also do I need to be a part of a college delegation to participate in the Film Festival?

No, the Film Festival is completely free to attend for anyone who is committed towards promotion of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. There are two modes of participation either as a part of an institutional delegation which needs to be communicated earlier to us through mail or by individual registration on the Google Form..

What are the other benefits and perks of attending the Film Festival?

The Film Festival has 2 segments:

  1. Pre & Post Festival Webinars and Expert Talks on individual SDG areas by Prominent Personalities.
  2. 2) The SDG Film Festival itself on 3rd and 4th October which would involve screening of award winning selected short documentaries and films on various SDG thematic areas. This shall also be followed by an address by each Documentary Director Live and Question and Answer session with the Delegates.

All registered delegates will receive prior communication to attend the Pre and Post Festival webinars apart from the Film Festival. Special Certificates shall be provided to Heads of Institutional Delegations and Participation Certificate to all registered Delegates of the Film Festival. A Post Festival speed networking session on zoom with all delegates is also expected to be organized by the Club.
How to register for the Film Festival and take part?

You can register by filling the form at Register or by clicking the Register Button on the website.
